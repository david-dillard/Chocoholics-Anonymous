Glossary

1) ChocAn: abbreviated form of Chocoholics Anonymous.

2) Member name: the name of a member, stated on their ChocAn card.

3) Member number: a member’s unique nine digit number, stated on their ChocAn card.

4) Suspended member: a ChocAn member who has had their account suspended for not paying membership fees for at least one month.

5) Provider: health care professional.

6) Provider number: a provider’s unique nine digit number.

7) Service code: specific six digit code in the healthcare industry used to refer to a specific medical service.

8) Provider directory: an alphabetically ordered list of service names and associated service codes and fees.

9) ChocAn Data Center: stores and verifies member numbers.

10) Main accounting procedure: reads the week’s file of services provided and prints the number of reports; run every Friday at the ChocAn Data center.

11) Acme Accounting Services: 3rd party organization who handles financial procedures relating to automated member payments for ChocAn.

12) Electronic funds transfer: often abbreviated EFT, refers to transferring funds from an electronic source.

13) Member report: a weekly document sent to members who consulted a ChocAn provider that week listing member name, member number, member street address, member city, member state, member ZIP code, and additionally for each service provided the following details are required: date of service, provider name, and service name.

14) Provider report: a weekly document sent to each provider who billed ChocAn during that week listing provider name, provider number, provider street address, provider city, provider state, provider ZIP code, total number of consultations with members, total fee for the week, and additionally for each service provided, the following details are required: date of service, date and time data were received by the computer, member name, member number, service code, and fee to be paid.

15) Summary report: sent to the ChocAn manager for accounts payable listing every provider to be paid that week, the number of consultations each had, and his or her total fee for that week.
